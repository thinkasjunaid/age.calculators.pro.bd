Age Calculation Logic

Age is calculated by subtracting the birth date from the target date with calendar-precise arithmetic. If the current month/day hasn't reached the birth month/day yet in the current year, one year is subtracted and the remainder is expressed in months and days using actual month lengths.

লিপ ইয়ার (Leap Year) Handling

If you were born on 29 February, our calculator correctly identifies leap years. In non-leap years, your birthday anniversary is counted on 28 February — consistent with official Bangladeshi age reckoning for NID and passport applications.
